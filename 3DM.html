Dear Client, 

Thank you for choosing the Developmental Milestones screener (3 Yeas old) to engage proactively in your child's development. As one of the many tiers I screener, the purpose of this age appropriated screener is to help you as parents to track your child's development. Among the early years of child development, it is crucial to identify potential development concerns early on during development. As part of the three tiers support systems, this screener is the beginning for us to alleviate your pressures as parents, to assess, assist and excel your child's development. 

The following screeners included both observational and interactive questions, meaning that it not only requires you as the screening administer to answers questions based on the observations of your child's daily behavior, but also to use interactive methods to acquire responses from you child (looking at pictures, pointing out items, etc.). 

At the end of the screening, there will be an interpretation component to explain some of the findings we have based on you and your child's responses. Along with the explanation, there will be follow-up intervention strategies and recommendations to address any of the concerns. 

At this point, you may continue with the screening when ready.
